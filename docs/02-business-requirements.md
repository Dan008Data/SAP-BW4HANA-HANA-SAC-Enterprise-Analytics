# Business Requirements

## Overview

The organization requires a centralized analytics solution to monitor sales performance across different business dimensions.

The goal is to provide business users with reliable and scalable reporting capabilities.

---

## Key Business Questions

The analytics platform must answer the following questions:

- What is the total sales order value?
- How many sales orders were created?
- What is the quantity sold per product?
- Which customers generate the most revenue?
- Which sales organizations perform best?

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Sales Order Quantity | Total quantity of products ordered |
| Sales Order Value | Net value of sales orders |
| Number of Sales Orders | Total count of orders |

---

## Reporting Dimensions

| Dimension | Description |
|---|---|
| Customer | Customer placing the order |
| Material | Product ordered |
| Material Group | Product category |
| Sales Organization | Sales region |
| Distribution Channel | Sales channel |
| Fiscal Period | Time dimension |

---

## Reporting Consumers

The reporting solution is consumed by:

- Sales Management
- Business Analysts
- Finance Teams
